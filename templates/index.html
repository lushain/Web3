The Problem We're Solving

Lack of Credit History

Millions in developing countries cannot access financial services due to the absence of formal credit history.

High Interest Rates

Traditional lenders charge exorbitant interest rates to the underbanked, creating cycles of debt.

Limited Funding Options

Rural communities lack access to diverse financial services and funding opportunities.

Our Solution

Our platform is a blockchain-powered, collateral-backed microfinance & credit scoring system designed to provide affordable loans to unbanked and underbanked individuals in rural India.

How It Works:

  • Alternative credit data and AI-driven risk assessment
  • Collateral-backed lending for security
  • Transparent, low-cost P2P lending through smart contracts
